JEE Key Points

Range is maximum at θ = 45°. Complementary angles (e.g. 30° & 60°) give the same range.
Horizontal velocity u·cosθ remains constant throughout (no air resistance).
At maximum height, vertical velocity = 0. Only horizontal component survives.
Trajectory equation: y = x·tanθ − gx²/2u²cos²θ — a parabola.
