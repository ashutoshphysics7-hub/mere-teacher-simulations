KEY POINTS

  • Period T = 2π√(m/k) is independent of amplitude — hallmark of SHM.
  • Velocity is maximum at equilibrium (x=0) and zero at extreme positions (x=±A).
  • Total mechanical energy E = ½kA² is constant (energy conservation in SHM).
  • Restoring force F = −kx always points toward the equilibrium position.
  • KE = ½m v², PE = ½k x² ; KE + PE = constant throughout motion.
