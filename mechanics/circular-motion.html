KEY POINTS

  • Centripetal force always points toward the center — it changes direction, not speed.
  • No work is done by centripetal force since it is perpendicular to velocity at all times.
  • Banking formula: tan θ = v²/(rg) — allows safe turning without friction.
  • Centrifugal force is a pseudo-force in the rotating frame; it does NOT exist inertially.
  • For vertical circle: min speed at top = √(rg) to maintain contact.
