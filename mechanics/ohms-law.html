KEY POINTS

  • Ohm's Law: V = IR — V vs I graph is a straight line through origin for ohmic conductors.
  • In series circuits, total R = R₁ + R₂ + … ; current is same through all components.
  • In parallel circuits, 1/R = 1/R₁ + 1/R₂ + … ; voltage is same across all components.
  • Power dissipated as heat in resistor: P = I²R (Joule heating).
  • Higher resistance at same voltage → lower current but more heat per unit current.
