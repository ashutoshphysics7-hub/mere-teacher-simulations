KEY POINTS

  • Real image: formed in front of mirror, inverted, magnification negative.
  • Virtual image: formed behind mirror, erect, magnification positive.
  • For concave mirror: f is negative (using standard sign convention).
  • For convex mirror: f is positive — always forms virtual, erect, diminished image.
  • When object is at focus, image forms at infinity (reflected rays are parallel).
