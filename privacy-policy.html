Privacy Policy Physics Lab

Last updated: May 2026  |  App: Physics Lab – JEE NEET Class 11  |  Package: com.mereteacher.physicsplay

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Physics Lab:

  • Phone number — used solely for OTP-based authentication via Firebase
  • Full name and class — for personalizing your learning experience
  • Usage data — anonymous analytics to improve app performance

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Verify your identity via phone OTP (Firebase Authentication)
  • Display your name and class within the app
  • Track learning progress and recommend content

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL database) and Firebase (authentication). Both services are enterprise-grade and GDPR-compliant. We do not store your OTP or authentication tokens beyond the session.

4. What We Do NOT Do

  • ❌ We do not sell your data to third parties
  • ❌ We do not show advertisements
  • ❌ We do not track your location
  • ❌ We do not share your phone number with anyone

5. Children's Privacy

Physics Lab is designed for students aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us data, contact us immediately.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication
  • Supabase — database and backend

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.
